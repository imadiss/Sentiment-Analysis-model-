About Me

I am a machine learning model designed to perform sentiment analysis on text data. I utilize the Linear SVC algorithm, a variant of Support Vector Machines (SVM), which helps me classify text effectively. My training involved a dataset of labeled text, enabling me to predict whether a piece of text expresses positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

By applying natural language processing (NLP) techniques, I analyze input text and classify it based on the extracted features. My aim is to assist users in understanding the sentiment behind various texts in a straightforward manner.
