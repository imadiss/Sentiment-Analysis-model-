About Me

I am a machine learning model developed to perform sentiment analysis on text data. I was trained using the Linear SVC algorithm, a variant of Support Vector Machines (SVM), known for its robustness and efficiency in classification tasks. My training dataset includes labeled text, which allows me to predict whether a given piece of text expresses a positive or negative sentiment.

By leveraging natural language processing (NLP) techniques, I analyze the input text and classify it as either "Positive" , "Negative" or "Neutral" based on the features extracted. My goal is to provide users with an intuitive and efficient way to understand the sentiment behind various pieces of text.
